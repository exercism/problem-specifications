Operators
---------
Bitwise:
  &   - and
  |   - or
  ~   - not
  <<  - shift left
  >>  - shift right
  >>> - shift right with zero fill

Logical:
  &&  - and
  ||  - or
  
Relational:
  >   - greater than
  <   - less than
  >=  - greater than equal to
  <=  - less than equal to
  !=  - not equal to
  ==  - equal to

Conditional/Ternary: (?:)
